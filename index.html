What is stenography?

Stenography

1: the art or process of writing in shorthand
2: shorthand especially written from dictation or oral discourse
3: the making of shorthand notes and subsequent transcription of them

Merriam-Webster.com

Captioners and court reporters are professionals who can type at speeds of 225 words per minute or more with at least 95% accuracy using a steno machine. We turn spoken words into written text using our machine and computer technology.

Being a captioner or court reporter is a unique and exciting career. Every day is different, and you get to learn new and interesting things all the time. Plus, it has the potential to be quite lucrative. Career opportunities include:

  • Broadcast Captioner (live television events and internet programs)
  • CART Provider (real-time transcription in educational, corporate, or community settings)
  • Court Reporter (court trials, examinations for discovery, and hearings)

But there’s a lot to learn beyond just typing well!
