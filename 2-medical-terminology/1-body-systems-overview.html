Body Systems Overview

  • Anatomy: study of body parts (structure)
  • Physiology: study of hot body parts work (function)
  • Pathology: study of the condition/process of a disease (health)

Levels of Organization

  • Cell
    • Microscopic living unit
    • All cells are similar structurally and functionally
      • Outer layer (cell membrane) that protects the cell and aids in cell-cell communication
      • Internal structures (organelles) that produce energy, make proteins and lipids, break down and transport material, and store genetic info
  • Tissue
    • Cells combine to make tissue
  • Organ
    • Tissues combine to make organs
  • Organ system
    • Related organs work together in a system
  • Organism
    • All the organ systems work together to create an organism
