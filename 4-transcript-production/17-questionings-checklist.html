Questionings Checklist

  • Title page
    • What type of proceeding is it?
    • Is it a continuation?
      • Last page number?
      • Last exhibit number?
      • Last undertaking number?
  • Include files
    • What are my include steno strokes?
      • BEGIN DISC (title page)
      • SWORN
      • EXHIBIT
      • UNDER (undertaking) vs. UADVISEMENT (undertaking taken under advisement) vs. UREFUSED (undertaking refused)
      • OBJECTION
      • OFF (off the record)
      • CONCLUDE EXAM
    • Was the witness sworn, previously sworn, or affirmed?
    • Concise & precise descriptions
  • Speaker guidelines
    • When to use Q or Q Speaker (after colloquy)
    • Are all speaker identifications inserted?
  • Punctuation
    • Check ending punctuation (statement or question?)
    • Is the punctuation correct?
    • Is transcript format followed consistently?
    • Does the transcript make sense?
  • Index
    • Are all elements included?
    • Are there any style/formatting errors?
  • Spell-check
  • Formatting and spacing
    • Are there words that should be kept together/hyphenated?
    • Two spaces after periods and colons, one after semi-colons and commas
    • Quotes
      • User defined paragraph 11 for 1st quoted indentation
      • User defined paragraph 12 for 2nd quoted indentation
      • User defined paragraph 20 for quoted testimony question
      • User defined paragraph 21 for quoted testimony answer
      • New line paragraph after quoted material
