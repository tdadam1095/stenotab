Multiparty Actions

  • Multiparty action: involves more than just a plaintiff, a defendant, and their respective counsel
    • Third parties, plaintiffs, defendants by counterclaim
    • Keep groups (e.g., all third parties) on the same title page, if possible
    • Keep all appearances together on one page
      • If split between pages, move entire appearances section to its own page

Considerations

  • Title page
    • List all parties involved, including plaintiffs, defendants, and any third parties
    • Add a new row to your table for each additional party
  • Appearances
    • Add a new row to the appearances table for each additional party
  • EZ speakers - wildcard
    • Add a new EZ speaker entry for each additional counsel (except SNAO and JAO)
    • Use wildcards

Scenarios

1. Multiple Defendants

  • Jane Do fell on an icy sidewalk outside a shopping complex with multiple storefronts, including Dental Clinic and Grocery Store
    • There is one plaintiff and two defendants

Style of Cause

- -
COURT FILE NUMBER: 2003 12345
COURT: COURT OF KING'S BENCH OF ALBERTA
JUDICIAL CENTER: EDMONTON
PLAINTIFF: JANE DO
DEFENDANTS: DENTAL CLINIC and GROCERY STORE

Appearances

- -
M. Bloggs For the Plaintiff
J. Public For the Defendant Dental Clinic
J. Major For the Defendant Grocery Store

EZ Speakers

  • SNAO = plaintiff counsel
  • JAO = defendant counsel
  • Wildcard = the remaining defence counsel

2. Third Party Defendant(s)

  • Grocery Store contracted Maintenance to clear the walkways of snow & ice
    • There is one plaintiff, two defendants, and a third party named by Grocery Store for having contributed to Jane Do's loss

Style of Cause

- -
COURT FILE NUMBER: 2003 12345
COURT: COURT OF KING'S BENCH OF ALBERTA
JUDICIAL CENTER: EDMONTON
PLAINTIFF: JANE DO
DEFENDANTS: DENTAL CLINIC and GROCERY STORE
THIRD PARTY: MAINTENANCE

Appearances

- -
M. Bloggs For the Plaintiff
J. Public For the Defendant Dental Clinic
J. Major For the Defendant Grocery Store
J. Smith For the Third Party Maintenance

EZ Speakers

  • SNAO = plaintiff counsel
  • JAO = defendant counsel
  • Wildcard 1 = the remaining defence counsel
  • Wildcard 2 = third party counsel

3. Second Questioner

  • Mr. Public, counsel for the defendant Dental Clinic, questions Jane Do
  • Then Mr. Major, counsel for the defendant Dental Clinic, questions Jane Do
    • Insert the sworn notation before the questioning by Mr. Major
      • "Previously Sworn"
    • Continued for each questioner
